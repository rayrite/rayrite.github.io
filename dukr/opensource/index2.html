Why Authentication Matters

Authentication systems are notoriously difficult to implement correctly. Subtle vulnerabilities in custom implementations can remain undetected for years, exposing applications to credential stuffing, session hijacking, and account takeover attacks.

By adopting community-vetted solutions with active maintenance and security review, you reduce technical debt and liability exposure from the project's inception.
