## üßÆ Math Support

The viewer includes full LaTeX math support through MathJax:

### Inline Math
Use single dollar signs: `$E = mc^2$` renders as $E = mc^2$

```latex
$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi}
```

- Subscripts: `x_1`
- Integrals: `\int`, `\sum`, `\prod`
- Matrices: `\begin{matrix}...\end{matrix}`
